In a land not so far away….
                                                     There lived a prince of Zerkanunu named Miguel. 
                                    Miguel was the son of king Darius and was prophesied to be the heir to the throne. 
                 Miguel was taught how to act like a king and fight like a warrior since he a youngling as he was to rule a nation 
                 mightier than what the world had ever witnessed .As custom, on the prince’s 18th birthday, he was to be sent away into the 
                 Forrest of Mischief, which was home to all the treacherous animals and the exiled murderous thieves known to the land of Zerknunu, 
                    and must return within a fortnite to prove to the king and the people of his kingdom that he was worthy of the crown.
